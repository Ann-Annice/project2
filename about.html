About Us

LifeLine Hospital is one of the largest and leading private hospitals in the region offering sophisticated diagnostic and therapeutic care in virtually every specialty of medicine and surgery. LifeLine Hospital specifically offers treatment to the needs of people of Thrissur, Palakkad and Malappuram districts, foreign tourists and foreign residents, treating thousands of patients each year. At LifeLine Hospital we have the enviable reputation of providing high quality and cost efficient hospital services, coupled with the warmth and caring nature.
Established in 1987 as a 40-bed hospital, LifeLine has now grown to a 300-bed Multi-Super Speciality General Hospital. Situated near Vadakkechira Corporation Bus Stand and Poonkunam Railway Station, LifeLine hospital is easily accessible by road or rail. From the very inception LifeLine was renowned for its TRAUMA care. It still holds it's pre-eminence in this field. We have over 50 doctors including about a dozen super specialists. ASWINI is appreciated for its excellence in patient care with a 'human touch'.
